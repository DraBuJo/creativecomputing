MUDD is an American lagerest beer sold by BOOMBOOM Company, established in NYC in 2008 and currently based in The New School.
Because the color of beer look like mud and people feel mud on eyes when they drink this beer, the brand name is MUDD
Establisher's son love green color. That's the reason why the tin is green color.
The logo which is behind the brand name is inspired by Japanese flag. It symbolize the raised sun.
The logo contains his son, John. He is playing the mud.

Children cannot drink it!

TMUDD is a 5.2% ABV NY-style wheat ale introduced under the name Spring Heat Spiced Wheat brewed in MUDD as a seasonal beer in 2006, then all year from 2007. The beer is brewed with wheat malt, two-row barley, orange, lemon, lime peel, coriander and Cascade and Willamette hops. MUDD won gold and bronze medals in the USA (White) category at the 2006 and 2007 North American Beer Awards, earning the reputation as America's Beer respectively.
